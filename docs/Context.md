# DeepNoise Project: Intelligent Acoustic Monitoring System

## Context
Modern industrial, urban, and technological environments produce large amounts of acoustic information. Machines, motors, alarms, tools, vehicles, and human-made systems generate sounds that can reveal useful information about their current state. For example, a machine may sound normal during regular operation, but its acoustic pattern may change when a component starts to fail, when friction increases, when an impact occurs, or when an alarm is activated.

## Project Goal
The team will design an intelligent acoustic monitoring system based on machine learning and neural networks. The system will receive short audio segments and classify them into different acoustic event categories.

## Requirements
- **Classification Problem:** Define at least four acoustic classes.
- **Scenarios:** Industrial, environmental, domestic, educational, or technological.
- **AI as Design Assistant:** Use generative AI to investigate, compare solutions, and propose a technically justified plan.
- **Critical Evaluation:** Evaluate, verify, and refine AI suggestions instead of accepting them blindly.

## Possible Acoustic Classes (Examples)
- Normal operation
- Metallic impact
- Friction or squeaking
- Alarm or warning tone
- Environmental or animal sounds
- Mechanical sounds or spoken commands
